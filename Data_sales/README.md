# Total Sales & Profit Analysis - Power BI Project

This Power BI project visualizes sales and profit data across multiple countries and categories using an Excel dataset. The project provides insights into sales performance, profitability, and trends over time.

## Dataset

The dataset contains profit data for various countries, broken down by year:

- **Columns:** Years, Austria, Belgium, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, Ireland, Italy, Netherlands, Norway, Portugal, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, United Kingdom, Total Result
- **Metrics:** Sum of Profit
- **Format:** Excel (.xlsx)

## Power BI Visualizations

The project includes the following dashboards and charts:

1. **Total Sales by Category and Segment**
   - Displays overall sales grouped by product categories and business segments.
   - Allows identification of high-performing categories and segments.

2. **Sum of Sales and Sum of Profits by Year**
   - Shows annual sales and profit trends.
   - Useful for analyzing year-over-year performance.

3. **Profit by Country**
   - Highlights profits generated in each country.
   - Allows comparison of profitability across regions.

4. **Profit by Region and Category**
   - Breaks down profit by geographical region and product category.
   - Useful for understanding regional performance in different categories.

## Key Insights

- Identifies top-performing countries and product categories.
- Shows trends in sales and profits over multiple years.
- Helps in strategic planning by highlighting areas of strength and weakness.
